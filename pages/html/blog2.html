How to put a screen protector or guard (step by step)

Installing a screen guard or protector can help protect your device's screen from scratches, smudges, and cracks. Here's a step-by-step tutorial on how to put a screen guard on your device:

Step 1:

Prepare the work area

Installing a screen guard or protector can help protect your device's screen from scratches, smudges, and cracks. Here's a step-by-step tutorial on how to put a screen guard on your device:

Step 2:

Clean the device screen

Use a microfiber cloth or cleaning wipe to gently clean the screen of your device. Make sure to remove any fingerprints, smudges, or dust particles.

Step 3:

Remove dust and debris

If your screen guard came with a dust removal sticker, carefully peel off the sticker from its backing. Gently press the sticker onto the screen and lift it up to remove any dust or debris that may be present.

Step 4:

Peel off the backing

Carefully peel off the backing from the screen guard, exposing the adhesive side. Be cautious not to touch the adhesive surface with your fingers as it may leave smudges or reduce the effectiveness of the adhesive.

Step 5:

Align the screen guard

Hold the screen guard by the edges or by the designated tabs (if provided) to avoid touching the adhesive surface. Align the screen guard carefully with the edges and openings of your device's screen. Take your time to ensure a precise fit.

Step 6:

Apply the screen guard

Once aligned, slowly lower the screen guard onto the screen, starting from one end and gradually working towards the other. Apply gentle pressure to the centre and smooth out any bubbles or creases using a soft card or squeegee. Work from the centre towards the edges to push out any trapped air.

Step 7:

Remove air bubbles

If any air bubbles remain, use the soft card or squeegee to gently push them towards the edges of the screen guard. Alternatively, you can try lifting the screen guard slightly and reapplying it if there are significant bubbles.

Step 8:

Final adjustments

Check the alignment and positioning of the screen guard. If necessary, carefully lift it from one corner and reposition it. Take your time to make any necessary adjustments before pressing down firmly on the screen guard.

Step 10:

Remove excess adhesive (if applicable)
